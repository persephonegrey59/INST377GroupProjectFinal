Thanks for using OpenWeatherApp! If you head over to our Our WeatherAPP Page, you'll be able to access real-time weather forecasts and maps for any city in the United States!

Our Mission

OpenWeatherApp was created to provide users with easy access to accurate and up-to-date weather information. Whether you're planning a trip or preparing for the day, we've got you covered.

How It Works

Simply enter your city and state initials on the Project Page, and our app will fetch real-time weather data from OpenWeatherMap's APIs. View detailed hourly and daily forecasts displayed in interactive charts or press 'Save Location' to store the location for future use!.
